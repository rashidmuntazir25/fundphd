Book a free counselling call.

Start with a free 30-minute counselling call. We get to know your situation, give you honest guidance, and decide together whether the funded-PhD route is right for you and whether we are a good fit to work together. We take on a limited number of people, so this call is how we both find out. No payment, no obligation.

What you get on the call

A free 30 minutes, no payment and no obligation. Bring your situation: your visa timeline, where you are in your studies, and what you are trying to do.

  • Honest assessment of whether the funded-PhD route fits your situation
  • Clear, practical guidance you can act on, whatever you decide next
  • We decide together whether you are a good fit for the programme
  • If it is a fit, we explain how it works and what it costs, privately, on the call
