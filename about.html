About

A method, not a sales pitch.

No funnels, no hype, no promises we cannot keep. A clear, proven system for turning a UK study visa into a funded PhD. Free onboarding call to see if it fits, no strings.

Fund PhD exists for one reason: most international students who want to stay in the UK are looking in the wrong place. They chase sponsorship roles in a market that has tightened sharply, while a fully funded PhD, a route that pays a stipend, waives fees, and extends their time in the country, goes unconsidered because no one has shown them how it actually works.

Universities will fund a strong enough profile. That profile can be built deliberately, from the outside.

The method is straightforward and repeatable. You build a competitive research profile by collaborating with academics who need technical contributions, turn that work into published papers, and convert that record into a funded place through a proposal, the right supervisor, and a strong application. It does not require a research post, insider connections, or luck. It requires the right sequence of moves, and the work to back them.

That is what Fund PhD teaches. We are not an admissions agency and we do not act on your behalf. We give you the system, proven in practice, and you execute it. The result is earned, not bought, which is exactly why it holds up.
