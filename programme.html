The Programme

Eight weeks to learn the path. Then the real work begins.

The path can be taught quickly. Walking it cannot. We give you the full system up front, stay reachable at the moments that count, and send you off equipped to do the twelve to eighteen months of work that actually earns the funded place.

A How it works

Taught once. Reachable when it matters.

The teaching is delivered up front so you are not waiting around for content to drip out. From there, you get a fixed, scheduled set of one-to-one touchpoints, not unlimited access, plus a private group to keep you accountable through the long stretch of building your record.

Deeper, hands-on help is available separately as add-ons, if and when you want it. The core programme is built so it never depends on us being on call at all hours, and so your progress never depends on our calendar.

B The eight weeks

A clear arc from lost to equipped.

Weeks 1–2The lay of the land

The path, end to end

Before anything else, you see the whole route. How funded PhDs actually work, what a competitive profile really looks like, and why the publication route is the lever most people miss. You finish these weeks knowing exactly what you are aiming at and why.

Weeks 3–4Finding your people

The collaboration engine

The core of the method. How to find PhD candidates and early-career lecturers working on things you care about, read their papers and theses, and spot the specific gaps you can help close.

  • Identify researchers worth approaching
  • Read for the gap, not just the topic
  • Build your own target shortlist
  • Outreach templates ready to send
Weeks 5–6The paper documents

Your CV, proposal and statement

We build the assets that carry your application. A drafted academic CV in the form that gets responses, and a first draft of your PhD proposal and personal statement that you will keep refining as your work matures.

  • Academic CV drafted and reviewed
  • PhD proposal first draft
  • Personal statement first draft
Weeks 7–8The admission playbook

Funding outreach, and your banked mock interview

The moves you will use later, taught now so you are never caught out. How to approach supervisors about funded opportunities, and a mock interview with feedback that you bank and redeem when you reach the application stage.

  • Supervisor funding-outreach playbook
  • One mock interview with feedback, banked for the admission phase
C After the eight weeks

Then it is on you.

When the programme ends, you have the path, the shortlist, the outreach, the CV and the proposal in hand. What you do not have is a shortcut around the work. The twelve to eighteen months of building your record is yours to walk.

If you want us back along the way, for deeper proposal work, more interview practice, or hands-on technical help, that is available as a paid add-on. But you are never obliged to buy more to make the core path work.
